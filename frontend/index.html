Why ChainSankalp?

Traditional voting systems often face challenges such as lack of transparency, delayed results, and vulnerability to manipulation. ChainSankalp addresses these issues by introducing a decentralized and tamper-proof solution.

Every vote is recorded on the blockchain, making it permanent, verifiable, and resistant to any unauthorized changes. This ensures trust not just in the system, but in the outcome.

Designed especially for students, this platform bridges the gap between technology and democracy — making participation simple, secure, and reliable.

Your vote stays completely private and encrypted
Once cast, your vote cannot be changed or deleted
Instant and transparent result calculation
Participate in voting from anywhere, anytime
Blockchain ensures auditability without revealing identity
Complete transparency with zero manipulation

Security First

Security is the backbone of ChainSankalp. Each vote is processed through smart contracts and recorded on a decentralized ledger, ensuring that no single authority can alter the outcome.

Your identity is verified through PRN and wallet mapping, while your vote remains anonymous — achieving the perfect balance between authentication and privacy.

With cryptographic protection and blockchain immutability, your trust is not assumed — it is guaranteed.

How It Works

Register using your PRN (Voter ID)
Connect your MetaMask wallet
Identity is securely verified
Cast your vote via smart contract
Vote stored permanently on blockchain
Results generated transparently

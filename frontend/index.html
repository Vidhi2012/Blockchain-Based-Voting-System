Why ChainSankalp?

In many voting systems, trust is often questioned. ChainSankalp changes that by using blockchain technology to ensure that every vote is counted exactly as it was cast.

Designed especially for students, it gives you the confidence that your voice truly matters.

🔐 Your vote stays completely private
⛓ No one can change your vote once cast
⚡ Results are fast and transparent
🌍 Vote from anywhere, anytime
🧾 Every vote is traceable (but anonymous)
👁 Full transparency, zero manipulation

Security First
